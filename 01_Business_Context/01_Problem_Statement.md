# Problem Statement

## Background

Healthcare contact centers receive a high volume of member and provider calls related to claim status, eligibility verification, benefits inquiries, provider lookup, pre-authorization requests, and service support.

Many of these requests are repetitive and require agents to manually authenticate users and retrieve information from backend systems.

---

## Current Challenges

* High volume of routine customer inquiries
* Longer wait times during peak hours
* Manual authentication and verification processes
* Increased operational workload for support agents
* Limited self-service capabilities
* Inconsistent customer experience across interactions

---

## Business Need

Healthcare organizations require a secure and scalable self-service solution that can automate common healthcare inquiries while protecting sensitive member information and maintaining regulatory compliance.

---

## Proposed Solution

Develop a HIPAA-compliant Healthcare Claims Voice Agent using Google Cloud CX Agent Studio and multi-agent orchestration.

The solution will authenticate users, identify customer intent, route requests to specialized agents, integrate with healthcare APIs, and escalate complex cases to human representatives when required.

---

## Expected Outcomes

* Reduced call center workload
* Faster query resolution
* Improved customer experience
* Increased self-service adoption
* Secure access to healthcare information
* Improved operational efficiency
