# Problem Statement

## Overview

Healthcare organizations receive a high volume of customer support calls every day from members seeking information about claims, eligibility, benefits, provider networks, and pre-authorizations. Most of these requests are repetitive and require agents to manually authenticate members before accessing healthcare information.

As the number of support requests increases, organizations face higher operational costs, longer waiting times, and increased workload on customer service representatives. These routine interactions consume valuable agent time that could otherwise be focused on resolving complex healthcare issues. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

---

## Business Challenges

The existing support process presents several operational challenges:

- High volume of repetitive healthcare inquiries.
- Longer customer waiting times during peak hours.
- Increased operational costs due to manual processing.
- Inconsistent customer experience across different support channels.
- Limited availability of human agents for handling complex healthcare cases. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

---

## Proposed Solution

To address these challenges, this project introduces a **Healthcare Claims Voice Agent** built using **Google CX Agent Studio**.

The solution securely authenticates members, identifies their intent, and routes conversations to specialized healthcare journeys such as Claim Status, Eligibility Verification, Benefits Inquiry, Provider Lookup, and Pre-Authorization. The agent also supports fallback handling and seamless escalation to a human representative whenever automation is unable to resolve the request. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

---


## Expected Outcome

The proposed solution aims to reduce manual effort, improve response times, provide consistent member support, and ensure secure access to healthcare information through authenticated and compliant conversations. By automating routine healthcare requests, organizations can improve operational efficiency while allowing human agents to focus on high-value and complex cases.
