# Assumptions

## Overview

The following assumptions have been considered during the analysis and design of the Healthcare Claims Voice Agent. These assumptions define the conditions under which the proposed solution is expected to operate successfully.

---

## Business Assumptions

### AS-01 Member Information Availability

It is assumed that valid member information is available and can be verified during the authentication process.

---

### AS-02 Backend Service Availability

It is assumed that all required backend services and mock APIs are available and return valid responses during the conversation.

---

### AS-03 Authentication Before Information Access

It is assumed that members must successfully complete authentication before any protected healthcare information is shared.

---

### AS-04 Accurate Intent Identification

It is assumed that the conversational agent can correctly identify the member's intent based on the information provided during the interaction.

---

### AS-05 Stable Network Connectivity

It is assumed that both the member and backend services have stable network connectivity throughout the conversation.

---

### AS-06 Voice Interaction

It is assumed that members communicate through a voice interface and provide clear responses during the conversation.

---

### AS-07 Human Agent Availability

It is assumed that a human support representative is available whenever the conversation requires escalation.
