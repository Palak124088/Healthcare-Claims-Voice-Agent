# Business Problem Statement

## Overview

Healthcare organizations receive a high volume of member support requests every day. Most of these requests involve routine healthcare services such as checking claim status, verifying eligibility, understanding plan benefits, locating in-network providers, and checking pre-authorization status.

These interactions are typically handled by customer support representatives, requiring manual member authentication and backend verification before healthcare information can be shared.

---

## Business Problem

The existing support process is heavily dependent on manual intervention, leading to longer customer wait times, increased operational costs, and repetitive workloads for customer support teams. As call volumes continue to grow, providing timely and consistent support while maintaining healthcare data security becomes increasingly challenging.

In addition, members expect faster access to information without waiting to speak with a representative. Manual processing also increases the possibility of inconsistent responses and reduces the availability of support agents for handling more complex healthcare issues.

---

## Proposed Solution

To address these challenges, the Healthcare Claims Voice Agent leverages Google CX Agent Studio to automate routine healthcare interactions through a secure conversational interface.

The solution authenticates members before accessing protected healthcare information, identifies the member's intent, routes the request to the appropriate healthcare journey, retrieves information through backend APIs, and provides accurate responses. When a request cannot be resolved automatically, the conversation is escalated to a human representative.

---

## Business Objective

The proposed solution aims to:

- Reduce manual effort for customer support teams.
- Improve response time for routine healthcare requests.
- Deliver a consistent conversational experience.
- Ensure secure access to protected healthcare information.
- Improve operational efficiency through conversational automation.
